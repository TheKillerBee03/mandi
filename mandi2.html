Christmas Letter

Dear Mom,
I just want say thank you—for everything and sacrifices you’ve made for me, and my biggest source of inspiration.

Thank you for always being there, no matter what. For your hugs when I needed comfort, and your patience even when I made mistakes. and I want you to know that I see it and cherish it deeply.

You’ve taught me the values of kindness, resilience, and hard work. I’m so lucky to have you as my mom, and I hope you know how much I love and appreciate you.

I promise to do my best to make you proud and to give back even just a fraction of the love and care you’ve given me. Thank you for being you—the most amazing mom anyone could ask for.

With all my love,
mandi
